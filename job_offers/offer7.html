About the job

Located in Northern California, the team at Joby Aviation is driven by our goal of creating an affordable, all-electric air transportation system. Imagine a piloted air taxi that takes off vertically, then quietly carries you and your fellow passengers over the congested city streets below, enabling you to spend more time with the people and places that matter most. Since 2009, our team has worked steadily to make this dream a reality. We’ve designed and tested many generations of prototype aircraft capable of serving in a network of electric air taxis. We’re looking for talented, committed individuals to join our team as we push onwards toward certifying the Joby aircraft with the Federal Aviation Administration, scaling our manufacturing, and launching our initial commercial service


As an integral member of our Factory Systems team in Munich, you will play a leading role in bringing the production of our eVTOL to Industry 4.0. You will be responsible for conception and development of services interacting with everything from large production machines to users and everything in between, driving automation and coherence to existing regulations in this new type of industry. With your development skills, you will drive the integration of our production machines and the connection to all related production data, significantly advancing automation to the greatest extent possible.

Be the pilot of your career and take off with and join the Joby team as:


(Senior) DevOps Engineer (m/f/d)


As a DevOps Engineer, you will play a pivotal role in ensuring the seamless operation of our global manufacturing facilities by designing and managing the build, test and deployment processes used in the Factory Systems team. This role is responsible for ensuring the smooth operation of our Kubernetes-based infrastructure as well as CI/CD pipelines and therefore bridging the gap between the development and the delivery of our components. Furthermore, you will be instrumental in setting up and maintaining the databases that underpin our services, developing a robust and scalable data infrastructure, including backup and mirroring processes, to support our growing global operations.

Our organization takes pride in its global presence, with facilities spanning multiple international locations. As such, the Factory Systems Engineering team is characterized by its international focus and collaborative spirit. We welcome candidates who are eager to contribute to a global team and thrive in a multicultural environment.


Your tasks in detail:
  • Design, implement and maintain our CI/CD pipelines
  • Manage and optimize our cloud infrastructure and services in multiple regions, including deployment, scaling and monitoring of microservices
  • Ensure the reliability and performance of our on-premises components, including VMs and containerized applications
  • Further enhance a fully integrated monitoring, alerting and logging system
  • Deploy, maintain and monitor various databases of the system, including backup and recovery strategy
  • Collaborate closely with developers to ensure stability and reliability of our products
  • Automate infrastructure-related tasks, using IaC tools


Your profile in detail:
  • Bachelor’s degree or degree in computer science or equivalent
  • Minimum 3 years of experience in a DevOps Engineering or similar
  • In-depth knowledge of containerization using Docker, Kubernetes, Helm etc.
  • Experience with CI/CD pipelines, preferably using GitHub Actions and ArgoCD
  • Proficiency in scripting languages, preferably Python
  • Well-versed in Infrastructure as code tools, such as Ansible or Terraform
  • Strong understanding of cloud computing, preferably with Azure
  • Familiarity with monitoring and logging systems, such as Prometheus and ELK
  • Solid understanding of security principles and best practices
  • Proven ability in deploying and maintaining various database types
  • Excellent communication and collaboration skills to use in our international work environment
  • Close familiarity with agile development methodologies and version control systems
  • Experience or general interest in a manufacturing environment is a plus


Our offer to you:
  • Long-term collaboration with permanent employment contract with 30 days vacation per year
  • Secure job in an international company that is supported by well-known investors
  • Varied challenges with the latest technologies and innovations
  • Flexible working hours and location (on-site & home office)
  • Encouragement of your willingness to perform and take responsibility with internal and external further training
  • Attractive salary package consisting of a fixed salary and employee participation in the form of restricted stock options
  • Subsidiarized Lunch (7 EUR per day from Mon-Fri)
  • Salary extra in the form of a voucher card
  • Public transport allowance or car travel allowance
  • Kindergarten allowance of Euro 100 per child
  • Regular (amazing) team events
